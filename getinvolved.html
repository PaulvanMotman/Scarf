VOLUNTEER WITH US

We are desperately searching for as many people as possible to help us out, whether with 'Saturday Sorting' or with coordination:

'Saturday Sorting': pretty simple - every Saturday we rock up to De Boemerang with a team of SCARF volunteers, boxes, tape and pens, and sort through the donations according to our sorting plan - women's jackets, men's jackets, children's jackets. women's tops/sweaters, men's tops/sweaters, boys tops/sweaters, girls tops/sweaters, so on and so forth.

Coordination:

  • volunteer coordination and scheduling for 'Saturday Sorting'
  • hunting for new volunteers
    • linking SCARF to other people doing similar work/looking for collaboration opportunities
  • coordination of shipping
  • finding new storage facilities

Don't have any clothes but want to help us out? Share this project with your friends and/or colleagues!
